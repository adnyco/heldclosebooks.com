Stories of Staying

Literature of presence.
Stories about the people who stay.

Held Close Books is an independent literary imprint dedicated to stories of caregiving, witness, and the quiet endurance of love. We publish a small number of deeply personal works — narratives born from lived experience, written from the inside of life's most difficult and most ordinary moments.

Presence Over Performance The most meaningful acts are often the quietest.
Witness Over Heroism Stories that observe honestly rather than glorify.
Literary Integrity Voice matters. Craft matters. Truth matters.
Compassion Without Sentimentality Honesty over spectacle. Lived experience over abstraction.
